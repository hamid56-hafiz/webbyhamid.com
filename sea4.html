Ertugrul Season 4 - Detailed Overview

Season Information

  • Premiere: October 25, 2017
  • Finale: June 6, 2018
  • Episodes: 91

Plot Highlights

  • The Return of Ertugrul: Ertugrul returns from captivity and stops his brother Dundar from selling their market.
  • Karacahisar Campaign: Gündüz is kidnapped; Ertugrul defeats the Byzantines and captures the Karacahisar fortress.
  • Ares’s Transformation: Tekfur Ares is captured, later converts to Islam, and becomes Ahmet Alp.
  • Palace Betrayals: Sadettin Köpek poisons the Sultan; Gıyaseddin becomes the new Sultan with Ertugrul’s support.
  • End of Noyan: Noyan and his sister Alangoya return but are defeated. Halime dies giving birth to Osman, and the tribe moves to Söğüt.

Main Characters

  • Ertugrul Bey: Leads his people through war and palace threats.
  • Dündar Bey: Misled by Köpek, he is banished by Ertugrul.
  • Sadettin Köpek: A cunning vizier, eventually executed.
  • Ares / Ahmet Alp: Converts to Islam and supports Ertugrul.
  • Noyan & Alangoya: Mongol enemies defeated by Ertugrul.
  • Halime Hatun: Dies after giving birth to Osman.

Themes

  • Loyalty vs. Betrayal: Seen in tribal politics and palace plots.
  • Faith and Redemption: Through Ares’s transformation.
  • Foundations of Empire: Osman’s birth marks the start of the Ottoman legacy.
  • Strategic Warfare: Military victories shape the tribe’s future.
